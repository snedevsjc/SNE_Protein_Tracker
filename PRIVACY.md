# Privacy Policy — Protein Tracker

**Last updated: 2026-05-25**
**Developer: SNE Interactive LLC**

## Summary

Protein Tracker does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All data you enter into the app stays on your device.

## What data the app handles

The only data Protein Tracker stores is what you enter yourself:

- Protein log entries (food name, grams, date, meal category)
- Your daily protein goal
- Your dark-mode preference

This data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and Apple's standard `UserDefaults` system. It is never transmitted to SNE Interactive LLC, never uploaded to a server, never shared with any third party, and never used for advertising or analytics.

## What we do not collect

We do not collect any of the following:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, Apple ID)
- Device identifiers (advertising ID, IDFV, IDFA)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Health data from HealthKit (the app does not request HealthKit access)
- Photos, contacts, microphone, or camera data

The app does not include any third-party SDKs (no Google Analytics, no Firebase, no Facebook SDK, no advertising networks, no crash reporters).

## Network access

Protein Tracker does not make any network requests. The only exception is when you use a subscription feature, in which case Apple's StoreKit handles the transaction directly with the App Store. SNE Interactive LLC does not receive or process payment information.

## Subscription

Protein Tracker offers an auto-renewable subscription:

- **Protein Tracker Pro** — $2.99 per year, after a 30-day free trial

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to those transactions. We do not have access to your payment information. You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time from Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.

## Children

The app is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Data deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, deleting it is as simple as either:

- Using the "Clear today's log" option in Settings
- Deleting the app, which removes all app data from your device

We have nothing to delete on our end because we never received your data in the first place.

## Changes to this policy

If we materially change how the app handles data, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance.
